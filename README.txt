We analyze the trend of virial infections in the US states after implementation of public vaccination.

1- wrangle-data.R reads the data from dslab and saves it in rdas subdirectry.
2- hepatitisA-analysis.R specifically analyses the trend of Hepatitis A across the country by state.
3- Hepatitis-report summerizes the findings and presents interesting graphs on the past and current situation 

The plots are saved in fig directory.
